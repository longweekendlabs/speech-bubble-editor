Editor

Built for quick visual edits, not generic design work.

The app focuses on the jobs people actually open it for: bubble placement, readable captions, layered media, and fast export.

Natural speech bubbles

Oval, cloud, rectangle, starburst, text-only, scrim, and caption styles with draggable tails and live styling.

Video-aware workspace

Open videos, scrub the timeline, trim, cut, reverse, slow down, and optionally mute audio while editing or exporting.

Inspector controls

Adjust fill, stroke, opacity, fonts, alignment, shadows, layer opacity, and tail width without hunting through menus.

Layered compositions

Add image and video layers, change order, select objects from the layer list, and arrange overlays on the canvas.

Meme and caption tools

Use classic meme mode or styled caption blocks when you need punchy text rather than a full speech bubble.

Full-resolution export

Export images as PNG, JPEG, or WebP and export edited video with FFmpeg bundled in official builds.

Workflow

Open media, add intent, export cleanly.

Open a photo or video

Drag media into the canvas or use the native file picker for your platform.

Add bubbles and captions

Pick a style, place it on the canvas, type directly, and drag the tail to the speaker.

Arrange and tune

Use the inspector, toolbar, layer list, and timeline tools to refine the composition.

Export

Save still images or render video edits with optional audio mute and timing changes.
